RECIPE FOR MAGGI

INGREDIENTS

  • water-1 glass
  • maggi-2 packs
  • maggi masala-2 sachets
  • green peas-2 tbsp
  • cooking oil-50 ml
  • garlic-5 pcs (slit)
  • onion-1 pcs(chopped)
  • tomato-1 pcs (chopped)
  • red chilli powder-1 tsp
  • garam masala-1 tsp
  • salt-1 tsp or as needed


PROCEDURE

  • Take a kadhayi and then add water , maggi noodles maggi masalas and green peas

    stir and cook basically

    we are making the two minute maggi here .

    once cooked keep aside

  • Take another vessel. add cooking oil, garlic, onion, tomato

    red chilli powder, garam masala, and salt then mix it

    well. tadka is ready now.

  • put off the flame add maggi to the tadka.

    fold the mixture gently.

    your tadka maggi is ready. serve hot.
